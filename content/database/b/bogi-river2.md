title: "Bogi River"
title_bn: "বগি নদী"
description: "This river originated from Vola river at Sarankhola Upazilla, Bagerhat that fall into Boleshwar river.
Length of this river is 3 km. width is 150 meters. Depth is 12 meters. Basin size is 12 sq. km.
This is seasonal river with continue flow. On the right bank of this river Sundarban is situated."
